MIGRAINE

Migraine is a neurological disorder which causes intense throbbing or a pulsing sensation in one area of the head and is commonly accompanied by nausea, vomiting and exteme sensitivity to light and sound. Migraine attacks can cause significant pain and last anywhere between 4 to 72 hours. The cause for migraine is not known but they have identified trigger factors which include lifestyle and hormonal changes.

Yoga - An adjuvant therapy

Stress, irregular sleep cycles and digital eye strain are common triggers of migraine treatment, regular exercise os frequently recommended along with conventional care.
